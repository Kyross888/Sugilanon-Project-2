# Lesson 4: Using the POS Terminal

## Overview
The POS Terminal is the heart of the system. This is where cashiers process customer orders and handle payments.

## What You Will Learn
- How to open and use the POS terminal
- How to add items to an order
- How to process a payment and complete a transaction
- How to handle order cancellations

---

## 4.1 Opening the POS Terminal
After logging in as **Staff** or **Admin**, navigate to the **POS Terminal** from the sidebar menu. This opens `pos_terminal.php`.

## 4.2 Browsing Products
- All available menu items are displayed with their **name**, **price**, and **category**.
- Use the **search bar** or **category filter** to quickly find items.
- Products with zero stock are automatically disabled.

## 4.3 Adding Items to an Order
1. Click on a product to add it to the current order.
2. The item appears in the **order panel** on the right.
3. Adjust the **quantity** using the `+` and `−` buttons.
4. To remove an item, click the **trash icon** beside it.

## 4.4 Applying Discounts
- Select a customer from the dropdown if they qualify for a **loyalty discount**.
- PWD/Senior discounts can also be applied from the order panel.

## 4.5 Processing Payment
1. Review the order summary (items, subtotal, tax, total).
2. Select the **payment method**: Cash, GCash, or Card.
3. Enter the **amount tendered** (for cash payments).
4. Click **Charge** to complete the transaction.
5. The system will display the **change** and generate a receipt.

## 4.6 Order Receipt
After a successful transaction:
- A receipt is displayed on screen.
- The receipt can be **printed** or saved as a PDF.
- The sale is automatically recorded in the database.

## 4.7 Cancelling an Order
- Click **Clear** or **Cancel Order** to remove all items from the current order before payment.
- Orders that are already processed cannot be deleted from the terminal — contact an Admin.

---

## Summary
The POS Terminal lets staff quickly build orders, apply discounts, process payments, and generate receipts — all in one smooth interface.

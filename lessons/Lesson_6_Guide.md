# Lesson 6: Sales Reports and Analytics

## Overview
The Sales Reports and Analytics modules give you detailed insights into your restaurant's performance across all branches.

## What You Will Learn
- How to view and filter sales reports
- How to read the analytics dashboard
- How to identify top-selling products
- How to export reports

---

## 6.1 Sales Reports (`salesreport.php`)
Go to **Sales Reports** in the sidebar. You can:

- Filter by **date range** (today, this week, this month, custom)
- Filter by **branch**
- Filter by **payment method** (Cash, GCash, Card)

### Report Columns
| Column | Description |
|---|---|
| Order ID | Unique order number |
| Date & Time | When the sale was made |
| Items | Products included in the order |
| Total | Amount collected |
| Payment | Method used |
| Cashier | Staff who processed the order |

## 6.2 Analytics Dashboard (`analytics.php`)
The Analytics page shows visual charts and summaries:

- 📈 **Revenue Trend** — Daily/weekly/monthly sales graph
- 🍜 **Top Products** — Best-selling menu items by quantity and revenue
- 💳 **Payment Breakdown** — Pie chart of Cash vs GCash vs Card
- 🏪 **Branch Comparison** — Revenue per branch

## 6.3 Dashboard KPIs (`dashboard.php`)
The main Dashboard shows at-a-glance stats:

- **Today's Revenue** — Total sales for the current day
- **Orders Today** — Number of completed transactions
- **Top Product** — Best-selling item of the day
- **Low Stock Alerts** — Items that need restocking

## 6.4 Exporting Reports
1. Apply your desired filters on the Sales Reports page.
2. Click **Export**.
3. Choose your format: **CSV** or **PDF**.
4. The file will download automatically.

---

## Summary
Sales Reports and Analytics give admins the data they need to make smart business decisions — from tracking daily revenue to spotting which menu items drive the most profit.

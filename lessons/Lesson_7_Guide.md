# Lesson 7: Customer Management

## Overview
The Customer Management module lets you store and manage customer information, which enables personalized service and loyalty tracking.

## What You Will Learn
- How to add and edit customer records
- How to view a customer's order history
- How customer data connects to the POS terminal

---

## 7.1 Opening Customer Management
Click **Customers** in the sidebar. This opens `customer.php`.

This section is accessible to **Admins**. Staff may view customer records during checkout.

## 7.2 Viewing Customer Records
The customer list shows:
- **Name**
- **Contact Number**
- **Email**
- **Total Visits / Orders**
- **Last Visit Date**

Use the **search bar** to find a customer by name or contact number.

## 7.3 Adding a New Customer
1. Click **Add Customer**.
2. Fill in the customer details:
   - Full Name
   - Phone Number
   - Email Address
   - Address (optional)
3. Click **Save**.

## 7.4 Editing Customer Information
1. Find the customer in the list.
2. Click **Edit**.
3. Update any information.
4. Click **Save**.

## 7.5 Viewing Order History
1. Click on a customer's name or the **View** button.
2. A panel shows all past orders with dates, items, and totals.
3. This helps track frequent visitors and their preferences.

## 7.6 Linking Customers to Orders (POS)
When processing a sale in the POS terminal:
1. Click the **Select Customer** dropdown.
2. Search for and choose the customer.
3. Their information is linked to the order for records and potential discounts.

---

## Summary
The Customer Management module helps Luna's Arrozcaldo build stronger relationships with their guests by keeping organized records and order histories.
